# App icons

These are used in the app; replace placeholders with your design assets for the correct look.

- **icon-pickaxe.png** – Pickaxe (logo). Used as the app logo in the Dashboard header and as the Mining tab icon in the footer. Replace the placeholder with your exact pickaxe image.
- **icon-notifications-bell.png** – Bell. Used in the Notifications screen header. Replace with your bell icon.
- **icon-buy.png** (optional) – Blue shopping bag for the Buy Minerals CTA on the Dashboard.
- **icon-sell.png** (optional) – Golden briefcase for the Sell CTA on the Dashboard.

The app already uses **icon-pickaxe.png** and **icon-notifications-bell.png** in code. Copy your final assets over the placeholder files to see your designs.

- **icon-mining-river.png** – Three wavy lines icon for "River/Alluvial" in Artisanal Location (MINING AREA TYPE).
- **icon-mining-mountain.png** – Mountain peaks icon for "Mountain/Hard Rock" in Artisanal Location.
